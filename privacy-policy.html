Privacy Policy for ChronoStride

Last updated: 22-04-2025

Introduction

ChronoStride ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to our mobile application.

Information Collection

ChronoStride is designed to function completely offline and does not collect any personal information. The app:

  • Does not require user registration
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not track user location
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not contain advertisements

Data Storage

All race timing data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers. This includes:

  • Race timing records
  • Race configurations
  • Event history

Device Permissions

ChronoStride requires minimal device permissions:

  • Wake Lock: To keep the screen active during timing sessions
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback during button presses

Data Deletion

All data can be deleted by:

  • Clearing the app's data through your device's settings
  • Uninstalling the application

Children's Privacy

ChronoStride does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
